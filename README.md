## 📝 Problem Statement

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a statistical measure used to evaluate the overall development of a country. It considers three major dimensions:

- **Health** – measured through Life Expectancy
- **Education** – measured through Years of Schooling
- **Standard of Living** – measured through Gross National Income (GNI) per capita

The goal of this project is to predict the Human Development Index of a country by analyzing these key indicators using Machine Learning techniques.

## 📌 Input Features

- **Life Expectancy at Birth** – Average lifespan of individuals
- **Expected Years of Schooling** – Number of years a child is expected to attend school
- **Mean Years of Schooling** – Average number of years of education received
- **Gross National Income (GNI) Per Capita** – Average income per person
